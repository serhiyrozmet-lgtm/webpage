WHAT IS NVQ?

NVQ is an official UK qualification that proves you can actually do your job to UK standards, based on real on-site experience.

Why you need NVQ
  • Confirms your professional level based on real work experience
  • Required to obtain Blue, Gold, and Black CSCS cards
  • Provides a clear career path from operative to senior roles (Supervisor / Manager)
  • Gives access to major construction sites across the UK with predictable pay and stable employment

HOW TO GET NVQ AND A CSCS CARD

We identify your trade, real work experience, the type of CSCS card you need, and the appropriate NVQ level. We analyse what you already have, what is missing, and create a clear, step-by-step action plan to reach the result ‚Äî without stopping work.

We register you in the system, set up the correct NVQ portfolio structure, and officially start the assessment process.

We explain the full assessment format and agree on how your experience will be confirmed. Together we define the process (photos, videos, documents), so you clearly understand every stage from start to finish.

Depending on the agreed plan, you provide the required evidence remotely, or an assessor visits you on site for an on-site assessment. Based on this, an official NVQ portfolio is created in line with qualification requirements.

In parallel with the assessment, you prepare for and pass the mandatory Health, Safety & Environment test, which is the second required step for obtaining a CSCS card.

After your portfolio is successfully approved, you receive your NVQ certificate and a confirmed Health & Safety test result. Based on these two documents, you apply for the appropriate CSCS card.
